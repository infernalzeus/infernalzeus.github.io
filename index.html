Background

About Me

I'm a Research Informatics Associate at Kent and Medway NHS and Social Care Partnership Trust, where I handle Information Governance (DPIA/GDPR), lead NLP system validation (AKRIVIA/CRIS), and build analytical extracts for clinical research studies.

Alongside this, I'm an Honorary Researcher at the University of Kent, working on the CHIP-D project — building the software pipeline for circadian rest-activity analysis from wrist-worn actigraphy devices.

I hold an MSc in Artificial Intelligence (Distinction) from Queen Mary University of London and a BEng in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, Goa. My prior work includes reinforcement learning for VR cognitive assessment at King's College London, and ML systems for precision agriculture.

I'm interested in projects at the intersection of AI, clinical data, and software engineering — particularly where the research is real and the stakes are high.
