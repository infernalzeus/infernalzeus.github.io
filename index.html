Background

About Me

I'm a Research Informatics Associate at Kent and Medway NHS and Social Care Partnership Trust, handling Information Governance (DPIA/GDPR), leading NLP system validation (AKRIVIA/CRIS), and building analytical extracts for clinical research.

Alongside this, I'm an Honorary Researcher at the University of Kent on the CHIP-D project — building the software pipeline for circadian rest-activity analysis from wrist-worn actigraphy devices.

I hold an MSc in Artificial Intelligence (Distinction) from Queen Mary University of London and a BEng in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, Goa. Prior work includes reinforcement learning for VR cognitive assessment at King's College London and ML systems for precision agriculture.

I've been awarded the National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) Scholarship (Top 0.02%) and led Enactus BITS Goa's Project Sivaar to a Top 12 Global Race-4-Ocean ranking.
